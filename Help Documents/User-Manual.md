## 1. General Navigation
The application uses a sidebar-based navigation system.
- **Switching Views**: Click any icon in the left-hand sidebar to switch between different modules.
- **Window Control**: 
  - **Drag**: Click and hold the top header (above the content) or the sidebar to move the window.
  - **Maximize/Restore**: Double-click the top header or click the square icon in the top-right.
  - **Minimize**: Click the minus (‚Äî) icon in the top-right.
  - **Close**: Click the (X) icon in the top-right.

## 3. Medical History & Supplements Tracker
*The Clinical record section for tracking patient history.*

### How to:
- **View History**: Select a client from the list at the top. Their historical assessments will appear in the tabs below.
- **Add New Assessment**: Click "‚ûï Add New," select the assessment date, and fill in the "Medical History" tab fields.
- **Track Supplements**: 
  - Go to the **Supplements Tracker** tab.
  - Select a supplement from the dropdown.
  - Enter Dosage, Frequency, and Notes.
  - Click "Add Item" to attach it to this assessment.

---

## 4. Anthropometrics (Measurements)
*Manage physical measurements and clinical markers.*

### How to:
- **Record Measurements**: Enter values for Height (cm), Weight (kg), Temp, SpO2, and BP.
- **BMI Calculation**: The system automatically calculates the BMI once Height and Weight are entered.
- **Clinical Markers**: Record Zinc Status and NOX Status using the dropdowns or text fields provided.

---

## 5. Body Systems Overview
*A comprehensive system-by-system health assessment.*

### How to:
- **System Assessments**: Provide detailed notes for each of the 21 body systems (e.g., Immune, Sleep, Digestion).
- **Navigation**: Use the "Section" tabs within this view to group related systems (Immune/Respiratory, Digestive/Energy, etc.).

---

## 6. Eye Analysis (Iris & Sclera)
*Specialized iridology and sclerology assessment tools.*

### How to:
- **Iris Mapping**: Assign values to constitutional types, pupil shapes, and specific markings (Nerve Rings, Radii, etc.).
- **Overlays**: Use the mapping tools to identify specific organ correspondences in the iris.
- **Save Photos**: If your system is connected to a camera/capture device, you can attach iris photos to the record (if enabled).

---

## 7. Diet & Nutrition
*Monitor the patient's daily nutritional intake.*

### How to:
- **Meal Tracking**: Document what the client consumes for Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and Snacks.
- **Lifestyle Factors**: Record water intake, exercise habits, and dietary restrictions.

---

## 8. Treatment & Impression
*Define the clinical diagnosis and the path forward.*

### How to:
- **Record Impression**: Summarize your clinical findings in the "Impression" box.
- **Set Expectations**: Document what the client expects from the treatment plan.
- **Plan Presentation**: Outline the steps of the immediate treatment plan.

---

## 9. Prescriptions
*Generate and manage patient prescriptions.*

### How to:
- **Create Prescription**: Combine clinical recommendations into a formalized list.
- **Instructions**: Add specific instructions (e.g., "Take with food") for each prescribed item.
- **Export**: Use the Reports view to print these prescriptions for the client.

---

## 10. Supplements & Distributors Inventory
*Manage your inventory of professional-grade supplements.*

### How to:
- **Add Supplement**: Enter the Name, Price, Category, and Stock Level.
- **Distributor Links**: Link each supplement to a distributor to track where products are sourced.
- **Inventory Reporting**: Generate lists of low-stock items or full inventory audits.

---

## 11. Reports Generation
*Creating professional PDF documents for your clients.*

### How to:
- **Select Sections**: Check the boxes for the sections you want to include (e.g., Anthropometrics, Diet, Body Systems).
- **Preview/Generate**: Click "Generate Health Report" or "Generate Prescription."
- **Save PDF**: Choose a location on your computer to save the document.

---

## 12. Scanned Notes
*Digitize and store external documents.*

### How to:
- **Upload Scans**: Click the "Upload" button to select PDF or Image files from your computer.
- **Category Tagging**: Tag documents (e.g., "Blood Test," "X-Ray") for easy retrieval later.
- **Viewing**: Double-click a record in the list to open the stored document in your default viewer.

---

**Tip**: Always remember to click **Save** after making changes in any view to ensure your data is written to the database!
